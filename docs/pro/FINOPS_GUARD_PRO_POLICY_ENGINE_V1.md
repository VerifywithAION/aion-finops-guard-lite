# FinOps Guard Pro Policy Engine V1

## Purpose

This document defines the Pro policy engine.

---

## Lite posture

Lite proves static policies and clear thresholds.

---

## Pro posture

Pro should support:
- policy inheritance
- service-specific rules
- role-based spend bounds
- project and org hierarchy
- override paths
- time-aware policy rules
- policy versioning
- staged rollout simulation

---

## Why it matters

A real enterprise system cannot rely on flat thresholds alone.

The Pro engine must turn policy into a governed operational layer.
