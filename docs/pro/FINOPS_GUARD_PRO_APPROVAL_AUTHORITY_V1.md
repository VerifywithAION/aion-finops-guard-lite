# FinOps Guard Pro Approval and Authority V1

## Purpose

This document defines how Pro should handle authority and approvals.

---

## Required capabilities

- approver identity chain
- delegated rights
- approval scope bounds
- stale approval invalidation
- revoked authority handling
- emergency override tracking
- approval receipts linked to decision receipts

---

## Why it matters

Spend authority should not be implicit.

It should be verifiable and admissible.
