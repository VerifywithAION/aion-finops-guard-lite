# FinOps Guard Pro Evidence Fabric V1

## Purpose

This document defines the evidence and observability layer for Pro.

---

## Required surfaces

- receipt registry
- decision explorer
- evidence timeline
- replay surface
- audit views
- policy diff views
- counterfactual explanation views
- linked spend and incident trails

---

## Why it matters

Receipts become far more valuable when they can be explored, replayed, and audited across time.
